🧠 How to Use Remox Tracker ?

Learn how each section helps you boost productivity and stay organized.

✅

Tasks

Tasks are basically your daily routines that can be different for each day, To use, click on the New Task button, enter the task name and its urgency, and add the task. You can also see some information in the cards above and to the side. for monthy and weekly mode first select your day then add your task.

Go to Tasks
💼

AnyList

Anylist is designed to add tasks you want to do over the months, in the chart you can see your daily and monthly progress, you can compare your months and...

Visit AnyList
📅

Habits

Habits show how consistently you have done your tasks according to Anylist. You can add or remove new habits from here. Note that the habits here and Anylist are the same.

Go to Habits
💻

Projects

Organize related tasks under one goal. Projects give you an overview of progress and deadlines. Perfect for structured planning.

Open Projects
🎯

Targets

Mark your Targets for next week , month or year and do all of them one by one.

Go to Targets
